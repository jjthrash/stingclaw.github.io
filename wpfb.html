LESSON 1: INTRO TO HTML

Welcome to HTML! HTML uses "tags," or words inside angle brackets (<>) The first things you will learn are the most basic tags that are required in every webpage. The first one, <html>, is the very basis for all other tags. It goes at the beginning and end of the webpage, like the sample below:

<html>
    <head>
    ...
    </head>
    <body>
    ...
    </body>
<html>

The tags <head> and <body>, which are second-level tags (they are the second 'tab' up), are also required. <head> is where you put the webpage title, styles (later), scripts (later), and <meta> (we will cover this soon) information. The <body> tag is where the webpage content goes.
